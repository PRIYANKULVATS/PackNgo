About PackNgo

About PackNgo 🚀

PackNgo is an integrated travel ecosystem — booking engine, AR/VR reviews, AI personal travel agent, real-time safety & crowd tracking and a social hub for solo travellers.

Founded in 2024, we're on a mission to revolutionize travel with cutting-edge AI and community-driven insights. Our team spans engineers, travel experts, and safety specialists.

Vision & Mission 🎯

To make travel safer, more personal and smarter using ML, AR and community-driven features.

Our Promise: Every traveler deserves a confident, safe, and personalized journey. We empower you with AI insights, real-time safety data, and a global community.

What We Provide 💎

  • ✅ AI powered personal travel agent
  • ✅ Real time crowd & safety tracking
  • ✅ AR + VR Travel Review
  • ✅ A Travel Buddy (solo traveler social network)
  • ✅ Smart Local Translator guide
  • ✅ Group Travel Suggestions
  • ✅ Travel Insurance & Emergency mode
